While I do like to think of myself as an open ocean there are specific hobbies, etc. I have interests in that make me, well, Keith

Art is Life

Drawing has always been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. As a child my mother always told me it was how I originally communicated to others due to me having a speech impediment. Even after I learning to talk art has always stuck with me throughout the years either by me creating my own or being exposed to other individuals works of art.

Video Games

For as long as I can remember video games have always been more than just an enjoying spare time activity when I want to simply have fun but also it's also done a great job with helping me unwind and destress (being an adult can have its moments so a break from reality never hurts every now and then).

Fitness

Over the years like many adults I've let myself go in the physical sense to the point where my health did take a bit of a hit. I found myself in a tough spot in life and I had to take control of my life before my situation worsened so I got serious about being more physically active. Strength training, engaging in cardio related activities, and other forms of physicl activity that aided in helping me not only cut down my weight but also helping me build discipline has helped me view life through different lenses.
